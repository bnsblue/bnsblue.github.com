Hi! I am Chang-Hong Hsu

I am a Ph.D. student majoring in Computer Science and Engineering, University of Michigan, Ann Arbor. I am a member of ClarityLab, together with a bunch of talented colleagues working with our awesome advisers Prof. Jason Mars and Prof. Lingjia Tang. My research interests include developing hardware and software techniques on highly parallel and reconfigurable platform for improving datacenter performance and energy efficiency.

Publication

Chang-Hong's current research interests include developing highly parallel, highly reconfigurable hardware and software architecture to improve datacenter efficiency for emerging workloads such as cloud application and machine learning algorithms. He has authored and co-authored 7 papers at top-tier conferences since 2010, in areas such as verifying correctness of hardware and software, and improving datacenter performance and power efficiency.
